Mobility as a Service

Putting city access into your hands

Teamwon - Matthew Bayles, Nathan Lant, Izzat Mukattash

About: This app was develop in Provo City, Utah. Students from Brigham Young University decided that transportation was not effecient enough, and they found a way to enhace the local economy while making it easier for the entire community to get from point A to point B with just a little more style.
Instructions: On the map, select your destination, starting from the Provo Trainstation. The app will then calculate the fastest route home and a route that is sponsored by a local business. The latter route will be more cost effecient but will take more time to reach your destination.
