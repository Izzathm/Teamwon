Mobility as a Service

Putting city access into your hands

First, go to the map and click "Select Origin", then click a starting point. Do the same for "Select Destination".




Once your points have been selected, filter your route based on an Uber, bus, or sponsored route.





Select "Get Routes" and compare the cost vs. time data for each option. You are also able to toggle on and off each of the different map options.





By selecting "Advanced Options" you are able to edit the controls, such as cost per mile or speed limit, making the results more accurate.
